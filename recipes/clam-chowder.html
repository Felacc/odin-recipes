Clam Chowder

Description

This delicious clam chowder (made with snails) is a staple comfort food in Hyrule. The perfect meal for when it's "one of those days."

Ingredients

  • Hearty Blueshell Snail
  • Goat Butter
  • Fresh Milk
  • Tabantha Wheat

Steps

  1. Find a cooking pot.
  2. If the fire under the cooking pot is not already lit, light it.
  3. Take all of the ingredients and hold them in your hands.
  4. Throw the ingredients into the un-oiled, likely-never-cleaned, indeterminate-temperature cooking pot.
  5. Sit back and watch as the magic pot produces the most beautiful clam chowder you've ever seen.
