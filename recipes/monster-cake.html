Monster Cake

Description

This exciting monster cake is a fun and surprisingly tasty sweet treat that you can make to show off your baking skills.

Ingredients

  • Monster Extract
  • Cane Sugar
  • Goat Butter
  • Tabantha Wheat

Steps

  1. Find a cooking pot.
  2. If the fire under the cooking pot is not already lit, light it.
  3. Take all of the ingredients and hold them in your hands.
  4. Throw the ingredients into the un-oiled, likely-never-cleaned, indeterminate-temperature cooking pot.
  5. Sit back and watch as the magic pot produces the most beautiful cake you've ever seen.
