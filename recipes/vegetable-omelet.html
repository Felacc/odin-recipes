Vegetable Omelet

Description

This vegetable omelet is a quintessential breakfast item to help you stay satiated on your journey through Hyrule.

Ingredients

  • Any vegetable, herb, or flower
  • Bird Egg
  • Goat Butter
  • Rock Salt

Steps

  1. Find a cooking pot.
  2. If the fire under the cooking pot is not already lit, light it.
  3. Take all of the ingredients and hold them in your hands.
  4. Throw the ingredients into the un-oiled, likely-never-cleaned, indeterminate-temperature cooking pot.
  5. Sit back and watch as the magic pot produces the most beautiful vegetable omelet you've ever seen.
